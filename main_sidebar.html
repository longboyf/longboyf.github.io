  • About Me
  • I was born September 23, 1979 in Mountain View, CA. After living in San Jose, California for 23 years, I decided to move to Las Vegas, Nevada. I attended the Universty of Nevada, Las Vegas and graduated with a degree in Business Marketing.

  • Work Experience

I traveled all over the country teaching casinos how to use our software. From there I moved to Zappos.com and worked in the Call Center for a year and a half and now I currently work as a Merchandise Planning Analyst.
